Frequently Asked Questions

What is the membership fee?

SHPE National membership fee is $30 for students. Chapter dues are waived for the first semester.

Who can join?

Any MDC student in STEM fields can join, regardless of ethnicity or background.

What is the time commitment?

We recommend attending at least two events per month to get the most out of your membership.

How can I get involved?

After joining, you can participate in projects, attend events, and apply for leadership positions.
